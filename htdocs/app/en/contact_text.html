For the purpose of updating the relevant particulars when compiling the Final Registers to be published in October every year. If there is any change in your principal particulars, please complete and sign the Application for Change of Registered Particulars form and submit it to the Home Affairs Department / Electoral Registration Officer (www.had.gov.hk/rre/en/electors) on or before 16 June of the same year.

Attention

Resident Representative Election

Under the existing legislation, if a registered elector of the Resident Representative Election (even if he/she is an indigenous inhabitant or a spouse/surviving spouse of an indigenous inhabitant) no longer resides in the Village for which he/she is registered, or his/her residential address in the Village is no longer his/her sole or main home, he/she is no longer eligible to remain registered as an elector, even though his/her name is still on the Final Register. It is an offence for such a person to vote in the election. Only eligible electors who still reside within the same registered Village have the right to vote in an election.

Kaifong Representative Election

Under the existing legislation, if a registered elector of the Kaifong Representative Election no longer resides in the Market Town for which he/she is registered, or his/her residential address in the Market Town is no longer his/her sole or main home, he/she is no longer eligible to remain registered as an elector, even though his/her name is still on the Final Register. It is an offence for such a person to vote in the election. Only eligible electors who still reside within the same registered Market Town have the right to vote in an election.
