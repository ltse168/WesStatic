Personal Information Collection Statement

Purpose of Collection

Personal data provided by you will be used by HAD only for the purpose of handling your enquiry.

Your enquiry may also be used for the compilation of statistics on the number and nature of enquiries received by HAD. The outcome of the resulting statistics will not be in a form that will identify your enquiry and any of your personal data.

The provision of your personal data is voluntary. If you do not provide sufficient information, HAD may not be able to process your enquiries.

 

Classes of Transferees

Where necessary, the particulars submitted by you may be provided to other departments/organisations/persons for the purposes of voter registration and election under relevant Ordinances and /or subsidiary legislation and all purposes related thereto.

 

Access to Personal Data

You have a right to request access to, and to request the correction of, your personal data and other related information you provided for the purpose of voter registration in accordance with the provisions of the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance.  Such requests should be made in writing to the Electoral Registration Officer at 30/F, Southorn Centre, 130 Hennessy Road, Wanchai, Hong Kong.
